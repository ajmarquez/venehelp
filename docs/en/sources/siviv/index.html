SIVIV — Sistema de Información de Víctimas

Use this to search for a relative in care centers.

Search tool for people affected by the earthquake, to find relatives admitted to health and care centers.

Active Volunteer Website Located people

It gathers listings of people in care centers, not missing-person reports. If the person is not in a health center, use the Missing people section.
